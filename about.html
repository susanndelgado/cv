Susan Nicole Arts

Susan Nicole Delgado

Susan Delgado, also known as Susan Nicole Arts, is a Cuban-American Fine Artist and Professional Designer. Grounded in the philosophy that "Dreams are the Foundation of Reality," her work focuses on the intersection of classical technique and imaginative storytelling. She is currently in a dedicated discovery phase, refining her craft across various subjects to define her primary artistic focus.

Fine Art & Studio Practice

A structured journey from imaginative concept to high-end gallery work.

The Discovery Phase

Exploring the intersection of classical foundations and fantasy themes. This time is dedicated to merging academic principles with surreal narratives.

Technical Mastery

Refining skills in Oils and Acrylics, moving from Procreate sketches to final execution on canvas.

The Antique Booth

To fund her practice and professional materials, Susan maintains an active booth at a local antique store. This serves as an outlet for technical studies and experimental pieces sold through the antique market.

Teaching & Resources

Susan offers private tutoring via Wyzant and shares her full "business of art" process on Patreon, including downloadable outlines and behind-the-scenes insights.
