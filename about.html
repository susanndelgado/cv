The Master's Process

Susan Nicole Arts is a sanctuary where classical technique meets modern innovation. From the initial spark of research in the Scriptorium to the final stroke of heavy-body acrylics, discover the alchemy behind every creation.

Phase I

Inquiry & Research

The journey begins in the Scriptorium, gathering lore, genomic philosophy, and historical references to ground the work in a deeper truth.

Phase II

Traditional Sketching

Capturing the raw energy. Graphite and paper allow for an organic flow that digital tools cannot mimic in the early stages.

Phase III

Digital Evolution

Refining mock-ups and line art within Procreate and Photoshop to perfect composition and color theory before the physical labor begins.

Phase IV

The Physical Transfer

Moving from pixels to weave. Using stencils or carbon transfer to map the digital blueprint onto the final canvas or board.

Final Act

Execution & Mastery

The application of heavy-body acrylics, oils, or enamel—bringing the initial research to its vibrant, tactile conclusion. This is where the alchemy is perfected and the vision is realized.
