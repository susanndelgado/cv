Stage I

Deep Research

Every masterpiece begins in the Scriptorium. Here, I gather lore, genomic philosophy, and historical references to ground the fantasy in truth.

Stage II

Traditional Sketching

The dance of graphite on paper. This is where the raw emotion and movement are first captured by hand.

Stage III

Digital Evolution

Using the tools of the modern age—Procreate for line art and Photoshop for mock-up modification—to perfect the composition before the first drop of paint is mixed.

Stage IV

The Canvas Transfer

Carbon paper transfers or vinyl cut stencils bring the digital precision back to the physical realm, ready for heavy-body acrylics.

Final Act

The Completed Work

The culmination of the Guild's efforts—a finished piece that lives between dreams and reality.
