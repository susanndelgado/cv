About Susan Nicole Delgado

Susan Nicole Delgado, also known as Susan Nicole Arts, is a Cuban-American fine artist from Brooklyn, New York. Grounded in the philosophy that "Dreams are the Foundation of Reality," my work focuses on the intersection of classical technique and imaginative storytelling. I am currently in a dedicated discovery phase, focusing on technical mastery and the refinement of my visual language.

Fine Art & Studio Practice

The Professional Process

My workflow is a structured journey designed to translate imaginative concepts into high-end gallery works, primarily executed in Oil. This includes extensive concept research, digital modification in Procreate or Photoshop, and preliminary color studies in watercolor or acrylics to finalize lighting and form before the final execution.

The Discovery Phase

I am currently exploring the intersection of classical foundations and fantasy themes. This time is dedicated to identifying the specific subject matter that will define my long-term professional portfolio, merging academic principles with surreal narratives.

The Antique Booth

To fund my practice and professional materials, I maintain a booth at a local Antique Store. This serves as an outlet for my technical studies, experimental styles, and vintage decor pieces that exist outside my core fine art portfolio.

Teaching & Resources

I offer private tutoring via Wyzant and share my full "business of art" process on Patreon. This includes downloadable outlines, video lessons, and behind-the-scenes insights into sustaining a multi-faceted art career.
