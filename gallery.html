SUSAN NICOLE DELGADO

Susan, a Cuban Artist was born in Brooklyn New York. She a very difficult child hood and developed Borderline Personality Disorder. Through her childhood art was her escape from reality her diary and her passion. She took a long time developing herself as a professional and discovered a joy for learning. She completed 180+ credits in college but only retained an Associates Degree due to switching majors. Although her background as a professional is multimedia programming and design Susan retains a strong interest in the fine arts.

Susan Perferred medium is oil however she works in acrylic and dabbles a bit in other mediums as well. Her Artistic Journey has put her in the position to explore a mangitute of subject mattershowever she dreams of crafting a stunning portfolio that weaves together the magical realm of fantasy realism with the elegant aesthetics of Renaissance Rococo and Baroque art. Her vision is to bring enchanting narratives to life through her unique artistic lens.
