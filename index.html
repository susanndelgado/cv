SUSAN NICOLE DELGADO

Susan Nicole Arts

Susan Delgado, also known as Susan Nicole Arts, is a Cuban-American fine artist from Brooklyn, New York. Following a career as a Graphic Designer and Front-End Developer, she is currently in a dedicated phase of artistic discovery. Her work spans a wide range of subjects as she explores the balance between technique and fantasy realism. While she has a deep-rooted preference for oils and acrylics, she is presently expanding into watercolors, color studies, and thumbnails. She is currently focused on refining her craft across various subjects—including still life and nature—as she works toward defining her primary artistic focus.
