Susan Nicole Delgado

Susan Delgado, also known as Susan Nicole Arts, is a Cuban-American Fine Artist and Professional Designer. Grounded in the philosophy that "Dreams are the Foundation of Reality," her work focuses on the intersection of classical technique and imaginative storytelling. While she has a deep-rooted preference for Oils, she is currently in a dedicated phase of artistic discovery, refining her craft across various subjects to define her primary artistic focus.
