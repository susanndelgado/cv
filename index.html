Susan Nicole Arts

Susan Nicole Delgado

Susan Delgado, also known as Susan Nicole Arts, is a Cuban-American Fine Artist and Professional Designer. Grounded in the philosophy that "Dreams are the Foundation of Reality," her work spans a wide range of subjects as she explores the balance between technique and fantasy realism. While she has a deep-rooted preference for Oils, she is currently in a dedicated phase of artistic discovery, refining her craft across various subjects to define her primary artistic focus.
